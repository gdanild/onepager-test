## 4) Visual & Interaction Specification

**Layout**  
Single vertical flow, generous spacing, strong sense of visual breathing room.

**Typography**  
Modern, calm, confident. Clear hierarchy without decoration.

**Color Palette**  
Light background, dark text, one restrained accent color.

**CTA**  
Subtle, always discoverable, single consistent style.

**Motion**  
Slow, smooth reveal on scroll. Soft hover states only.

**Mobile Behavior**  
Fully responsive. CTA visible immediately. No clutter.
